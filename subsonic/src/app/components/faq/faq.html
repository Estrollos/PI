Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some of the most common questions about Subsonic. If you have a question that is not answered here, don't hesitate to contact us.

Tickets

Where can I buy tickets for Subsonic Festival?

Tickets are available exclusively through our official website. We recommend purchasing early, as tickets sell out fast!

Are tickets refundable or transferable?

All ticket sales are final and non-refundable. However, tickets are transferable — you can change the attendee name through your account up to 48 hours before the event.

I didn't receive my ticket after purchase. What should I do?

Please check your spam/junk folder first. If you still can't find it, contact our support team at support@subsonicfestival.com with your order number.

Is there a ticket limit per person?

Yes, a maximum of 4 tickets per person per order to ensure fair access for all fans.

Event Info

What are the festival dates and location?

Full details are available on the Event Info page.

What happens if the festival is cancelled or postponed?

In the event of a cancellation, all ticket holders will receive a full refund. If the event is postponed, your ticket will remain valid for the new date.

Access and ID

Do I need to bring ID to the festival?

Yes, you must bring a valid photo ID that matches the name on your ticket. Entry may be refused without it.

Can I leave and re-enter the festival grounds?

Yes, re-entry is allowed on the same day with a valid wristband. Lost wristbands will not be replaced.

Accessibility

Are there accessible tickets or facilities available?

Absolutely. We offer accessible viewing areas and dedicated facilities. Please select the accessibility option during checkout or contact us directly for assistance.

Digital Tickets

Do I need to print my ticket?

No, digital tickets on your phone are perfectly accepted. Make sure your screen brightness is turned up when scanning at the entrance.
